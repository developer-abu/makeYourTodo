#Project Name:
makeYourTodo
#Project Purpose
We often make plans for the next day or for the future and write them down.
This habit helps us stay disciplined and understand whether we are committed to our plans or not.

This website helps you note down your plans and manage your tasks easily.

#How to use 
click on let's start button
click on cross sign (a popup massage for mobile user)
use desktop mode if mobile user 
a form will be opened 
fill every filled like Your name, current date completed by(due date),
job tittle, job description(only 200 char is allowed)
click on add button 
click on check box if completed 
click on delete last task if a task is added ny mistake.
click on delete button if want to delete all the task
click on print if you want to print 
#why use this ?
no sign up 
your data will be saved at local storage, 
no data will be removed until you click on delete button 
your data will be  visible always in the site as it is saved at local storage of your browser
