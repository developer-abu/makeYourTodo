#Project Name: <br>
makeYourTodo<br>
#Project Purpose<br>
We often make plans for the next day or for the future and write them down.<br>
This habit helps us stay disciplined and understand whether we are committed to our plans or not.<br>

This website helps you note down your plans and manage your tasks easily.<br>

#How to use <br>
click on let's start button<br>
click on cross sign (a popup massage for mobile user)<br>
use desktop mode if mobile user <br>
a form will be opened <br>
fill every filled like Your name, current date completed by(due date),<br>
job tittle, job description(only 200 char is allowed)<br>
click on add button <br>
click on check box if completed <br>
click on delete last task if a task is added ny mistake.<br>
click on delete button if want to delete all the task<br>
click on print if you want to print <br>
#why use this ? <br>
no sign up <br>
your data will be saved at local storage, <br>
no data will be removed until you click on delete button <br>
your data will be  visible always in the site as it is saved at local storage of your browser<br>
